About JNS Construction

A licensed, field-led contractor committed to clear communication, written scopes, and work that holds up — serving Palm Harbor and surrounding Pinellas and Pasco County.

Who We Are

JNS Construction Services LLC is a Florida certified residential contractor building a reputation on reliability, transparency, and quality execution. We serve homeowners and light commercial clients throughout Pinellas and Pasco County — from Palm Harbor and Dunedin to Tarpon Springs, Clearwater, and New Port Richey.

We started with a simple belief: construction clients deserve a contractor who communicates clearly, scopes the work in writing before anything starts, and doesn't disappear once the deposit clears. That principle shapes every project we take on, regardless of size.

Every job is handled under Florida Certified Residential Contractor License CRC1334879, issued and verified through the Florida Department of Business and Professional Regulation (DBPR). We carry the required insurance and maintain full compliance with Florida building codes and permitting requirements.

How We Work

Our process is designed to eliminate the uncertainty that makes construction stressful. Every project — from a targeted repair to a full renovation — follows the same structured approach.

1

Free Site Consultation

We visit your property, assess conditions, listen to your goals, and discuss realistic timelines and budget expectations. No charge — this is part of how we build an accurate estimate, not a sales call.

2

Written Estimate & Scope

Before any work starts, you receive a detailed written proposal — what's included, what materials will be used, what's explicitly excluded, and the full cost. No vague allowances. No hidden extras discovered mid-job.

3

Permitting & Pre-Construction

Where permits are required, we handle the full process — application, plan submission, review coordination, and inspection scheduling. We don't start construction until the permitting picture is clear.

4

Supervised Construction

Field-led execution with consistent progress communication. Issues get surfaced and addressed as they come up — not after they've snowballed into change orders and schedule slips.

5

Final Walkthrough & Close-Out

We walk the finished project with you, address any punch-list items, obtain final inspections where applicable, and confirm the work matches the written scope before the project is closed out.
