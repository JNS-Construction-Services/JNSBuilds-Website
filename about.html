About JNS Construction

A licensed, field-led contractor committed to clear communication, written scopes, and work that holds up — serving Pinellas County and surrounding areas.

Who We Are

JNS Construction Services LLC is a Florida certified residential contractor serving homeowners and select commercial clients throughout Pinellas County and surrounding areas. We built this company on one principle: clear communication, written scopes, and no disappearing after the deposit clears.

Every project is handled under Florida Certified Residential Contractor License CRC1334879 — active, insured, and verifiable through the Florida DBPR.

How We Work

Every project follows the same structured process — whether it's a targeted repair or a full renovation.

1

Free Site Consultation

We visit your property, assess conditions, and discuss realistic timelines and budget expectations. No charge — it's how we build an accurate estimate, not a sales call.

2

Written Estimate & Scope

You get a detailed written proposal before any work starts — what's included, what materials are specified, what's excluded, and the full cost. No vague allowances.

3

Permitting & Pre-Construction

Where permits are required, we handle the full process — application, plan submission, and inspection scheduling. Construction doesn't start until the permitting path is clear.

4

Supervised Construction

Field-led execution with consistent progress updates. Issues get addressed as they surface — not after they've turned into change orders and delays.

5

Final Walkthrough & Close-Out

We walk the finished project with you, handle any punch-list items, and confirm the work matches the written scope before close-out.
