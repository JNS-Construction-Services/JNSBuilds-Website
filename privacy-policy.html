Privacy Policy

How JNS Construction Services LLC collects, uses, and protects information provided through this website.

Last updated: March 17, 2026

JNS Construction Services LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website located at jnsbuilds.com (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights with respect to that information. By using the Site, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide voluntarily through our estimate request form, including:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Description of the service or project needed

We do not collect payment card information, government IDs, or sensitive financial data through this website.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information collected through the estimate request form is used solely to:

  • Contact you regarding your project inquiry or estimate request
  • Schedule a site visit or consultation
  • Communicate about the scope, pricing, and status of your project

We do not use your information for marketing to third parties, sell your data to data brokers, or use it for any purpose unrelated to fulfilling your service request.

3. Form Submission Processor (Formspree)

Our estimate request forms are processed by Formspree (formspree.io), a third-party form submission service. When you submit a form, your information is transmitted to Formspree's servers and forwarded to us by email at projects@jnsconstructionfl.com. Formspree's privacy practices are governed by their own documentation at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy. We recommend reviewing it if you have concerns about how your data is handled in transit.

4. Google Maps Embed

Our Contact page includes an embedded Google Maps iframe that shows our business location. Loading this map may cause your browser to send information (such as your IP address) to Google's servers. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy (policies.google.com/privacy). You can decline to load the map by using a browser extension that blocks iframes or third-party requests.

5. Cookies and Tracking

The Site itself does not set cookies or use tracking scripts. However, third-party services embedded in the Site (Google Fonts, Google Maps) may set their own cookies or make tracking requests pursuant to their own privacy policies.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With service providers who process data on our behalf (e.g., Formspree) and are bound by confidentiality obligations
  • If required by applicable law, court order, or government regulation
  • To protect the safety, rights, or property of JNS Construction Services LLC, our clients, or the public

7. Data Retention

We retain inquiry and project communications for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfill the purpose for which they were collected or as required by applicable Florida law. When information is no longer needed, we delete or destroy it using reasonable methods.

8. Children's Privacy

This Site is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information through the Site, contact us at the address below and we will delete it promptly.

9. Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to request:

  • Access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion of your information from our records

We will respond to reasonable requests within 30 days.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Site after any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
