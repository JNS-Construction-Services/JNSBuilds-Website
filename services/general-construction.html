General Construction

Licensed residential and light commercial construction — new builds, room additions, structural framing, and permit-managed projects across Palm Harbor and Pinellas County.

Built Right from the Ground Up

When you're breaking ground on a new project — whether that's a room addition, a complete new build, or structural work that has to be done correctly the first time — you need a contractor who shows up, communicates, and executes. JNS Construction Services LLC brings field-led oversight to every general construction project, with licensed management, written scopes, and a process built around your timeline.

All general construction work is performed under Florida Certified Residential Contractor License CRC1334879, ensuring every project meets state code requirements and is backed by a properly licensed contractor. We serve homeowners and light commercial clients throughout Pinellas and Pasco County.

  • Permit application and management included where required
  • Field-supervised execution — not just subcontracted and handed off
  • Regular progress updates and clear communication start to finish
  • Final inspection coordination and project close-out documentation

Our Process

Every general construction project follows a defined process so nothing falls through the cracks and you always know where things stand.

1

Site Consultation

We visit your property, assess the existing conditions, review your goals, and discuss your timeline and budget expectations. No fee — this is included in the estimate process.

2

Written Scope & Proposal

A detailed written proposal is provided outlining exactly what work will be performed, the materials involved, what's excluded, and the full cost. No vague line items or surprise allowances.

3

Permitting & Scheduling

Where permits are required, we submit the application and coordinate with the county. We schedule trades and material deliveries around inspection milestones to avoid delays.

4

Construction Phase

Field-supervised execution with regular progress updates. We manage the job site, coordinate all trades, and address issues as they arise — not after the fact when they've compounded.

5

Final Walkthrough & Close-Out

We walk the completed project with you, document and address any punch-list items, obtain final inspections where applicable, and confirm everything meets the agreed scope before the project closes.
