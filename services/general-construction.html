Our Process

Every general construction project follows a defined process so nothing falls through the cracks and you always know where things stand.

1

Site Consultation

We visit your property, assess the existing conditions, review your goals, and discuss your timeline and budget expectations. No fee — this is included in the estimate process.

2

Written Scope & Proposal

A detailed written proposal is provided outlining exactly what work will be performed, the materials involved, what's excluded, and the full cost. No vague line items or surprise allowances.

3

Permitting & Scheduling

Where permits are required, we submit the application and coordinate with the county. We schedule trades and material deliveries around inspection milestones to avoid delays.

4

Construction Phase

Field-supervised execution with regular progress updates. We manage the job site, coordinate all trades, and address issues as they arise — not after the fact when they've compounded.

5

Final Walkthrough & Close-Out

We walk the completed project with you, document and address any punch-list items, obtain final inspections where applicable, and confirm everything meets the agreed scope before the project closes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do room additions and structural work require a permit in Pinellas County?

Yes. Room additions, structural framing changes, and new builds require a building permit in Pinellas County. JNS Construction Services LLC handles the full permit process — application, plan submittal, required inspections, and final sign-off. We don’t start structural work before a permit is in hand.

How long does a typical construction project take?

Most single-room additions run 8–14 weeks from permit approval to final walkthrough, depending on scope, material lead times, and inspection scheduling. We provide a project timeline in the written proposal before work begins so you know what to expect before signing.

Will you subcontract the work, or does your crew handle it?

We use a mix of in-house crews and vetted trade partners depending on scope. Field supervision is provided by JNS Construction on every project — you’ll have one point of contact and one accountable party from kickoff through close-out.

What does your written estimate include?

Our proposals itemize the scope of work, specified materials, project exclusions, schedule outline, and total contract price. There are no open-ended allowances or vague line items. You know exactly what you’re agreeing to before signing anything.
