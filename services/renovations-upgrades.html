Our Renovation Process

Renovations require more coordination than most people expect. Our process keeps everything moving in the right order and at the right time.

1

Discovery & Site Walk

We walk the space with you, understand what you want to change and why, identify any hidden conditions (moisture, structural issues, outdated materials) that could affect scope or budget, and set realistic expectations.

2

Scope Writing & Proposal

A written proposal is prepared that details every line of work — demolition, rough-in, installation, finishes, and allowances — with a firm total price. No open-ended estimates.

3

Selections & Material Procurement

We coordinate with you on material selections, confirm lead times before scheduling, and procure or verify materials are on-hand before starting demo. This prevents midproject delays.

4

Build Phase

Demolition, rough work, inspections (where required), and installation are executed in proper sequencing with experienced tradespeople under field supervision. You receive updates at each major milestone.

5

Punch List & Completion

We walk the completed renovation with you, document any items requiring touch-up or correction, complete them within an agreed timeframe, and formally close out the project.
