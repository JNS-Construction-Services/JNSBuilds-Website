- Public URLs: extensionless (`/about`, `/contact`)  
- Files on disk: still `about.html`, `contact.html`  
- 301: `/about.html` ‚Üí `/about` (all inner pages)  
- Canonical + sitemap: extensionless  
- Internal nav: extensionless links (optional but cleaner)

**Option B ‚Äî If you require `.html` in the address bar**  
- 301: `/about` ‚Üí `/about.html` (all extensionless ‚Üí `.html`)  
- Canonical + sitemap: `.html`  
- Expect 2‚Äì8 weeks for GSC URLs to migrate; temporary ranking fluctuation possible
